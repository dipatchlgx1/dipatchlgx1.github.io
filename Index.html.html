Frequently Asked Questions

What is concrete?+
Concrete is a construction material composed of cement, water, sand, and gravel. When hardened, it forms a solid and durable mass used in foundations, walls, roads, and bridges.
How do I get a quote?+
Contact us via our online form, call 512-552-5589, or email us. We'll discuss your needs and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote.
How long does a project take?+
Smaller residential projects like patios or driveways typically take 1–2 days. Larger commercial projects may take several weeks. We provide a clear timeline during planning.
What type of concrete do you use?+
Our standard mix has a minimum strength of 3,000 PSI, with high-strength options exceeding 6,000 PSI for commercial and industrial applications.
Do you offer warranties?+
Yes, we offer warranties on all concrete work. We guarantee the quality of our materials and workmanship and are committed to resolving any issues.
Do you handle permits and local codes?+
Absolutely. We stay current with all Austin and Travis County codes and handle required permits for your complete peace of mind.
Do you do custom design projects?+
Yes! We specialize in custom projects and love creating unique designs — from special decorative finishes to one-of-a-kind concrete structures.
